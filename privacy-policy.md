# Privacy Policy – Magic Atlas

Magic Atlas respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
- Account information used for authentication (such as email address)
- Purchase information handled securely by Apple’s App Store
- Notification preferences
- Anonymous diagnostic data for app stability

## How We Use Information
We use this information only to:
- Provide access to your account
- Enable app features such as Daily Ritual reminders
- Process purchases through Apple
- Improve app reliability and performance

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## Data Security
All authentication and purchase processing is handled using Apple and Firebase secure services.

## Your Choices
You can delete your account at any time from within the app.
